A collection of poems spanning twenty years — about survival, freedom, grief, and the small moments that carry us through.

I.

Here I am. Take me.
Tortured, I listen to the rain
Hard and heavy it falls
beating to the pulse of my heart
awakening my soul.

— from "The Falling Rain"

About

Christina
Rosie

A writer and poet whose work spans twenty years of living, surviving, and finding language for the things that resist it. These poems move between grief and joy, silence and alarm, darkness and the light that insists on rising.
