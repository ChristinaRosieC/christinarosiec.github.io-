A collection of poems spanning twenty years — about survival, freedom, grief, and the small moments that carry us through. READ THE COLLECTION → I. Here I am. Take me. Tortured, I listen to the rain Hard and heavy it falls beating to the pulse of my heart awakening my soul. — FROM "THE FALLING RAIN" SELECTED WORKS The Collection 01 The Falling Rain Here I am. Take me. Tortured, I listen to the rain Hard and heavy it falls... SPIRIT → 02 Destination Pending... I collect my dreams in cupped hands. I carry them close afraid they may fall and shatter. JOURNEY → 03 Never Knew I never knew when. I never knew where. Sinking, breathing, the deeper I go... DISCOVERY → 04 Sound the Alarm We're dying out here, a silent pandemic, where the weakest of the herd... RESISTANCE → 05 Unedited It doesn't happen overnight. They don't hand you a red pen and ask you to cross yourself out. RECLAIMING → 06 Edited It didn't happen overnight. I didn't hand them a red pen, I made the corrections myself. THE OTHER SIDE → 07 Stardust I've been there. I've felt the thunder. I've ridden the storm, I've been taken under. ENCOURAGEMENT → 08 Promise Before memory — falling to the ground felt purposeful. Part of something greater, calm as one. HOPE → 09 The Breakthrough The breakthrough, like the opening of a secret thread, the one that matters, the one that is. TRIUMPH → 10 Stall Stare at me now. I choose my direction. Trailing the gifts of this incredible journey. TRANSCENDENCE → 11 Believed The tears flow, and the hope of being believed is fading. If I died right now, would you change your mind? GRIEF → 12 Miner I am grief. Not the kind that whispers. I dig. LOSS → 13 Unnoticed Did anyone notice when the rope snapped? When the water submerged — CHILDHOOD → 14 To Sight Head above water just. Looking over the edge, I hold on. FLIGHT → 15 The Oneness Relax into change — or continue the same? Only my strength propels me... SPIRITUAL → 16 Love Me For Me I see the look of despair when you face me in the mirror. Even the sun feels cruel...
